The great thing about the Salesforce platform is its ability to be customized.
You can start with some basic capabilities and customize the platform to address
the changing needs of your business.

<!--more-->

Customer Relationship Management (CRM) platforms have traditionally, been
implemented to help companies track and store their key business contacts and
the activities and opportunities associated with these. The use of a CRM has
expanded far beyond this and CRM’s are more commonly used as a business platform
to perform **Marketing Automation**, **Proposal and Contract Management**,
**Advanced Reporting and Analytics**, **Inventory and Financial Management**.
The Salesforce Platform provides both basic and advanced capabilities to help
companies grow beyond the traditional use of a CRM. Some Examples of Salesforce
Capabilities:

### Marketing

Provides the ability to do automated lead tracking and assignment to capture all leads
and provide an easy way to respond if the lead has an immediate interest in your
company’s products or services, or to nurture the lead who is not quite ready to
make a buying decision. Helps develop a variety of campaigns to keep your clients
informed of new products and services, news updates and thought leadership.

### Sales Proposals

You can manage the proposal process directly in Salesforce so that you can
present client documents such as quotes and proposals from Salesforce. Ttrack
and manage status according to your sales process without having to go into
another system to inform your clients.

### Customer Support

Manages customer inquiries and support issues directly from Salesforce so that
you can have a complete view of your customer and can make future product and
service decisions based on the insight you have available across **Sales and
Support** in Salesforce.

### Analytics and Reporting

The data maintained within Salesforce can be turn easily into reports. You can
create basic dashboards for the core data you maintain in Salesforce for example
pipeline, average deal size, lead status, campaign reporting, etc. Dashboards
are easy to build, customized and can be refreshed so that you can see the
status of your business in real time and gain insight in a nice clean
dashboard view.

### Applications to further expand your CRM Capabilities

The great thing about the Salesforce platform is its ability to be customized.
You can start with some basic capabilities and customize the platform to address
the changing needs of your business. One of the most impressive and powerful
tools available to Salesforce customers is the **AppExchange** where you have a
variety of free and paid applications that integrate with Salesforce to further
enhance your use of Salesforce.
